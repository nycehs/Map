Key metrics: the last 7 days of data

The daily test rate, percent positivity, and number of people who test positive can help show how significantly the pandemic is affecting a neighborhood - and how concerned to be. For example, if a neighborhood has a low testing rate but a high percent positivity, that can indicate a troubling situation. Or, high numbers of people testing positive can be due to a higher testing rate.
